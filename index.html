KOENIGSEGG GEMERA

The World's First Mega-GT

Extreme performance meets spacious luxury in a revolutionary four-seat hypercar that defies all conventions.

REVOLUTIONARY DESIGN

The Gemera is Koenigsegg's first four-seater, designed to carry driver and passengers alike in comfort and style without compromising the extreme performance that defines the brand.

MEGA-GT EXPERIENCE

The Gemera combines extreme megacar attributes with the ability to comfortably seat four large adults with their luggage. Despite its spacious interior, it delivers mind-blowing performance, innovative technology, and a driving experience unlike any other four-seater on the planet.

  • Four full-size seats with ample space
  • Dihedral synchro-helix doors for easy access
  • Three electric motors combined with TFG engine
  • 1,700+ horsepower and 3500 Nm of torque

REVOLUTIONARY POWERTRAIN

The Gemera's powertrain combines Koenigsegg's innovative 2.0-liter three-cylinder twin-turbo Freevalve engine (the Tiny Friendly Giant, or TFG) with three electric motors. This hybrid setup delivers an astonishing 1,700+ horsepower and 3,500 Nm of torque, allowing for a 0-100 km/h time of just 1.9 seconds.

Combustion Engine 600 HP
Electric Motors 1,100 HP
Combined Output 1,700+ HP

INNOVATIVE FEATURES

Freevalve Technology

Camshaft-free valve actuation allowing independent control of each intake and exhaust valve for optimized performance and efficiency.

Dihedral Synchro-Helix Doors

Revolutionary door mechanism that rotates and extends outward and upward simultaneously, providing easy access to both front and rear seats.

Koenigsegg Direct Drive (KDD)

Single-gear direct drive transmission system eliminating the need for a traditional gearbox, reducing weight and improving efficiency.

All-Wheel Drive & Steering

All-wheel drive with torque vectoring and all-wheel steering for unparalleled handling and stability at all speeds.
